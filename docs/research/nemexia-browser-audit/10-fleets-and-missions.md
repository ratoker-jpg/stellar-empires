# 10 — Fleets and missions

Historical Help defines twelve regular-planet missions (Transport, Espionage, Attack, Stationing, Colonization, Recycling, Pirating, Astroploitation, Sun Support, Space Trip, Renegade Espionage, Renegade Attack) and documents team-planet-only mission types separately. These names do not fully match current Horus labels (for example Dislocation/Pirate Raid/Gas Extraction), so `data/missions.json` preserves both lists without claiming an exact mapping.

Flights, Battles, and Simulator are visible navigation targets from Shipyard (`CONFIRMED_UI`). The Flights landing page lists owned-unit quantities and these mission options: Transport, Espionage, Attack, Dislocation, Colonization, Recycling, Pirate Raid, Gas Extraction, Sun Support, Space Flight, Renegade Espionage, and Renegade Attack (`CONFIRMED_UI`).

A mission was not started; destination, fuel, timing, validation, cancellation rules, and outcomes remain `ACTION_REQUIRED`. Do not dispatch a fleet to discover them.
