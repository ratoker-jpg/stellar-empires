# 04 — Planet and zones

`CONFIRMED_UI`: Planet shows planet score categories, ozone capacity/current amount, population capacity/current amount, planet overview, and galaxy coordinate. Three top-level zones are Resource, Industry, and Military.

The accessible screens establish a hub → zone information architecture. Building names, upgrade costs, build times, and queue behaviour were not read from cards; they are `UNKNOWN`. Do not infer them from Stellar Empires’ implementation.
