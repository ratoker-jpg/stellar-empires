# 05 — Resources

## Historical Help cross-check

`CONFIRMED_HELP`: Metal, Minerals, and Gas are the three economic resources. Help describes their use for buildings, ships/defence, upgrades, and science. Energy powers buildings; Ozone affects Energy production; Scrap is created after destroyed ships; Population represents hangar space used by ships and defence. It further says departing flights normally do not release origin hangar space, except Stationing. These are source-backed concepts, not a tested current-server formula or cap.

Resource Zone displayed hourly production: Metal **350**, Minerals **250**, Gas **100** (`CONFIRMED_UI`, capture-time values). It also displayed Ozone maximum **15,000** and current Ozone **14,860** (`CONFIRMED_UI`).

The UI links to gathering and storage information. It also presents premium purchase links; these were not opened. Resource caps, consumption, production formulas, storage progression, and premium conversion are `UNKNOWN`.
