# 13 — Social and meta

The persistent navigation includes Alliance, Personal, Ranking, Tutorial, Discord, Forum, Support, Auction, Arena, and premium purchase entry points (`CONFIRMED_UI`). Their internal features were not opened.

Single-player adaptation: preserve the information architecture—rank/achievement feedback, explicit progression goals, and opt-in social references—but replace live alliance, trading, auctions, and chat with deterministic NPC, local-save, or informational equivalents (`INFERRED`).
