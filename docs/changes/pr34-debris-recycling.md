# PR #34 — Debris, plunder and recycling

Schema v9 stores persistent debris fields by planet. Every destroyed ship or defense converts 30% of its metal and crystal build cost into debris; half of a destroyed attacking fleet's carried metal and crystal also remains in orbit. Repeated battles merge into the same field.

A surviving attacker that wins may plunder up to 10% of each target resource while respecting remaining cargo capacity. Battle reports now include debris and plunder totals. A recycling mission requires an Aegis recycler, collects metal then crystal up to free cargo capacity, updates or removes the field and returns automatically. The mission screen exposes recycling, debris totals and expanded battle reports.
