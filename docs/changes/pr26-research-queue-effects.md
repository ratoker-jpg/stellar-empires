# PR #26 — Research queue and real effects

Research is now a playable deterministic queue. Starting a technology validates the laboratory and dependency graph, reserves planet resources, schedules a serialized completion event and blocks a second concurrent research. Cancellation removes the event and refunds 75% of the reserved cost.

Completed technologies update the owning empire exactly once. Engineering Systems shortens future construction times and Energy Networks increases reactor output during every economy refresh and offline time segment. The Research screen shows costs, requirements, queue progress, cancellation and launch actions.
